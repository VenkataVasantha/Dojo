  • Why Your Online Reputation Makes A Difference
  • July 17, 2012Dex One Contributor
  • According to a 2012 survey bySteel House,an e-commerce, 98 percent of shoppers said that online customer reviews have a major influence on their decision to purchase a product or service. The survey also found that 72 percent of consumers claimed they always read reviews before making a purchase. Most respondents read up to 10 reviews about a product or service before making a decision. So it's more important than ever to monitor what's being said about your business online.
